
### Usage

1. Allow camera and microphone permissions when prompted
2. Switch between photo and video modes using the toggle
3. Tap the capture button to take photos or start recording
4. Use pause/resume controls for video recording
5. Access your gallery via the top-right button
6. Download or delete media items as needed

## Deployment

This app can be deployed to any static hosting platform:

- [Vercel](https://vercel.com) (Recommended)
- [Netlify](https://netlify.com)
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com)
- [Firebase Hosting](https://firebase.google.com/docs/hosting)

## Browser Support

- Chrome 60+
- Firefox 55+
- Safari 11+
- Edge 79+
